About Us

The Medical Village Project is a visionary healthcare and community development initiative designed to provide accessible, high‑quality medical services, education, research, and economic growth in Nigeria and West Africa.

Our Vision

To build a world‑class sustainable Medical Village delivering affordable healthcare, advancing medical education and transforming community wellbeing across Africa.

Our Mission

  • Accessible healthcare services
  • Train future health professionals
  • Promote preventive medicine
  • Create jobs and innovation
  • Operate sustainably

Telemedicine Platform

Before the physical hospital opens in 24 months, Medical Village will operate digitally through the internet providing virtual care.

  • Video consultation with doctors
  • Pharmacist prescription guidance
  • Laboratory coordination
  • Home care support
  • Ambulance coordination
  • Weekly doctor education sessions

Benefits

  • Reduce hospital queues
  • Lower medical costs
  • Prevent diseases early
  • Improve access to healthcare
  • Faster appointments
